Nutritional Deficiencies and Related Diseases/Disorders Dataset

Identifier: (NutriData uri)

Title: NutriData Nutritional Deficiencies and Related Diseases and Disorders

Description: This dataset contains data from the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation, namely, from the GBD section, Global Burden of Disease. The dataset focuses on nutritional disorders and deficiencies of individuals whose age mainly ranges from 5 to 19 years old (adolescents).

Modified: 20/11/2021

Source: IHME-GBD_2019_DATA-27a144e6-1.csv, IHME GBD API (link to the source). The dataset was created as a merge from various dataset (asthma, iron deficiency, vitamin A deficiency, diabetes mellitus II, protein energy malnutrition, liver-related diseases) downloaded from the IHME-GBD API (for reproduction: all locations; all years; context: cause; years: 0-19; metric: percentage; measure: incidence;)

Format: CSV, JSON

Periodicity: Annual

Child mortality rate 5-14 years old

Identifier: (Nutridata uri)

Title: NutriData Child Mortality

Description: This dataset contains data from the World Health Organization and from the UN IGME agency. It contains estimates, calculated by dividing the number of deaths by the population at risk during a certain time frame, on children between 5-14 years old.

Source: child-mortality.csv (link to the source), UNICEF-CME_DF_2021_WQ-1.0-download.csv, (link to the source)

Theme: Child mortality and burden of disease

Format: CSV, JSON

Periodicity: Annual

Rights Holder: WHO, UN IGME

Subject: The probability that a child aged 5 dies before reaching its 15th birthday, a rate of deaths per 1000 children.

Publisher: Amanda Culoma, Giulia Massimino, Ariele Santello, Andrea Schimmenti

Creator: Amanda Culoma, Giulia Massimino, Ariele Santello, Andrea Schimmenti

Language: English

Daily per capita Supply of Kilocalories

Identifier: (Nutridata uri)

Licence: NutriData Daily per capita Supply of Kilocalories

Description: This dataset (sources: FAO and Our World in Data) contains the supply of kilocalories per person per day. Daily caloric supply is defined as the average per capita caloric availability. It indicates the caloric availability delivered to households.

Source: daily-per-capita-supply-of-calories.csv, (link to the source)

Theme: Daily per capita supply of kilocalories per person
