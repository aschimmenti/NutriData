Nutritional Deficiencies and Related Diseases/Disorders Dataset

Identifier: (NutriData uri)

Title: NutriData Nutritional Deficiencies and Related Diseases and Disorders

Description: This dataset contains data from the Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation, namely, from the GBD section, Global Burden of Disease. The dataset focuses on nutritional disorders and deficiencies of individuals whose age mainly ranges from 5 to 19 years old (adolescents).

Modified: 20/11/2021

Source: IHME-GBD_2019_DATA-27a144e6-1_ISO-ready.csv, http://ghdx.healthdata.org/gbd-results-tool

Theme: Malnutrition, diseases, disorders

Format:CSV (comma-separated values), JSON

Periodicity: Annual

Rights Holder: IHME – Institute for Health Metrics and Evaluation, Global Burden of Disease

Subject: Number of people from 5 to 19 years old who suffer nutritional deficiencies and related diseases (e.g. Asthma, Cardiovascular diseases, Diabetes mellitus etc.)

Publisher: Amanda Culoma, Giulia Massimino, Ariele Santello, Andrea Schimmenti

Creator: Amanda Culoma, Giulia Massimino, Ariele Santello, Andrea Schimmenti

Language: English

Temporal Coverage: 2010-2018

Malnutrition Estimates Dataset

Licence: (Nutridata uri)

Licence: NutriData Children Malnutrition Estimates

Description: This dataset contains data from UNICEF, WHO & World Bank’s Joint global database on child malnutrition. It estimates the number of children who show symptoms and effects of under- and malnutrition. The indicators (trends) are four: overweight, stunting, wasting (and severe wasting) and underweight.

Description: (when we update the website)

Source: malnutrition-estimates.csv, https://data.unicef.org/resources/dataset/malnutrition-data/ (stored in Unicef website)

Theme: Child Malnutrition by indicators

Format: CSV, JSON

Periodicity: Annual

Rights Holder: UNICEF, WHO, World Bank

Subject: Country-level trends of 4 core child malnutrition indicators, along with national estimates and other ones such as geographic location, age and sex.

Publisher: Amanda Culoma, Giulia Massimino, Ariele Santello, Andrea Schimmenti

Creator: Amanda Culoma, Giulia Massimino, Ariele Santello, Andrea Schimmenti

Language: English

Temporal Coverage: 2000-2020

Spatial Coverage: World

Licence: (same as URL)
